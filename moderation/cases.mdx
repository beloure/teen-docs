# Cases & Mod Stats

Teen tracks every moderation action so you can review history and hold moderators accountable.

## Mod Stats

View how many actions each moderator has taken.

```
,modstats
,modstats @moderator
```

This shows a breakdown of bans, kicks, timeouts, and other actions taken by a specific moderator — or a leaderboard of all moderators.

## Case Logging

All moderation actions (ban, kick, timeout, mute, etc.) are automatically recorded. If you have [Logging](/setup/logging) configured, cases are sent to your moderation log channel with:

- **Case number**
- **Action type** (ban, kick, timeout, etc.)
- **Moderator** who performed the action
- **Target** user
- **Reason** (if provided)
- **Timestamp**
